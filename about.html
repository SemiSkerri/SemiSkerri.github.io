Making The Perfect Plant Soil!
Depending on your type of plant, the type of soil they require will vary. Most typical houseplants
do fantastically in a chunky soil though, so always have some on hand! It is easy to make and doesn't
break the bank! You only need a few ingredients to whip up a medium that your plants will love!

A Nice Chunky Soil
