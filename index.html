My name is Alexandra Haines and I have been collecting orchids and other plants for almost ten years. Through those years I have received and lost many specimines, leading to me end up writing all the care instructions for each one down to refer back to, and even that is sometimes not enough. Each species of plant has specific needs and wants, some like it wet while others like it more on the dry side. Some love lots of direct light while others will burn easily if in direct contact with the sun as they grow under the canopy in tropical areas. I love strange and differnt types of plants, some of my collection smell like chocolate while others smell like parmesan cheese and they come in all sorts of shapes and colors. By compiling these plants together I hope to make care for them easier and share what I have learned with others.
