First you must making the perfect plant soil!

Depending on your type of plant, the type of soil they require will vary. Most typical houseplants do fantastically in a chunky soil though, so always have some on hand! It is easy to make and doesn't break the bank! You only need a few ingredients to whip up a medium that your plants will love!

A Nice Chunky Soil

Depending on your plant and preference the amount of each substrate can be changed, however a good base is a 1:1 ratio of each mixed thoroughly.

This video with Wild Fern does not use any soil in her chunky soil. Once you get into the hobby you will find out what you perfer. Some people are over water-ers and require more perlite and bark to make sure the plants do not rot, while others are under water-ers and require more soil or coco coir to ensure their plants do not overly dry. There are many different soil combinations and many depend on the type of plant you are using it with and the type of plant parent you are. I would suggest making small batches of different types of soil and see what works best for your life-style and types of plants!
